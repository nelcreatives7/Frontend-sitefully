Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your website project with Sitefully.

General Questions

What services does Sitefully offer?

We specialize in building modern, secure, and scalable websites using Django. Our services include website design and development, e-commerce platforms, web applications, branding, SEO optimization, and ongoing maintenance.

Do you only work with Django?

Django is our main backend framework due to its reliability and security. However, we also integrate modern frontend tools such as Tailwind CSS, React, and Alpine.js for responsive user interfaces.

Can you redesign or upgrade an existing website?

Yes, we can revamp your current website to make it modern, mobile-friendly, and optimized for performance and conversions while migrating your data safely.

Project Process

How does your web development process work?

Our process includes discovery, design, development, testing, and deployment. We keep you involved at every step to ensure your vision is realized.

How long does it take to build a website?

Standard websites take 3–5 weeks; complex sites like e-commerce or web apps may take 6–10 weeks. We provide a timeline after consultation.

Do I need to provide content for the website?

Yes, but we also offer professional content writing and design services if needed.

Pricing & Support

How much does a website cost?

Pricing depends on size and functionality. Small business sites start at KSh 25,000, while custom sites are quoted individually.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes. We offer maintenance plans covering updates, backups, security, and support after launch.

Can I request updates or new features later?

Absolutely. Your website is scalable and can be updated as your business grows.

Technical Questions

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are 100% responsive for desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Do you handle domain registration and hosting?

Yes, we can manage domain and hosting setup or help configure your existing hosting.

Is SEO included?

Yes, we follow best SEO practices including metadata, load speed, and mobile optimization.
