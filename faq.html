Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your website project with Sitefully.

General Questions

What services does Sitefully offer?

We specialize in building modern, secure, and scalable websites using Django. Our services include website design and development, e-commerce platforms, web applications, branding, SEO optimization, and ongoing maintenance.

Do you only work with Django?

Django is our main backend framework due to its reliability and security. However, we also integrate modern frontend tools such as Tailwind CSS, React, and Alpine.js to ensure fast, beautiful, and responsive user interfaces.

Can you redesign or upgrade an existing website?

Yes, we can revamp your current website to make it modern, mobile-friendly, and optimized for performance and conversions. We’ll migrate your data safely while improving the overall experience.

Project Process

How does your web development process work?

Our process includes five key phases: discovery, design, development, testing, and deployment. We keep you informed and involved at every step to ensure your vision is achieved exactly as planned.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timelines depend on the project’s complexity. A standard business website typically takes 3–5 weeks, while more advanced platforms (like e-commerce or web apps) can take 6–10 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after consultation.

Do I need to provide content for the website?

Yes, we encourage you to provide your content (text, images, and branding). However, if you need assistance, we offer professional content writing and graphic design services to help you stand out online.

Pricing & Support

How much does a website cost?

Our pricing depends on your project’s size and functionality. Small business sites typically start from KSh 25,000, while custom or e-commerce websites are quoted individually based on scope and requirements.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Absolutely. We provide affordable maintenance plans covering updates, backups, security monitoring, and ongoing support to ensure your site runs smoothly after deployment.

Can I request future updates or add new features later?

Yes! Your website is built with scalability in mind. You can always contact us to add new pages, features, or integrations as your business grows.

Technical Questions

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all websites we build are 100% responsive. They automatically adjust for desktops, tablets, and smartphones, providing a seamless experience across all devices.

Do you handle domain registration and hosting?

Yes, we can manage your domain and hosting setup for you. Alternatively, if you already have hosting, we’ll help configure it for smooth deployment.

Is SEO included in your web development services?

We follow best SEO practices during development — including fast load speeds, metadata setup, and mobile optimization — to ensure your site is ready for search engines from day one.
